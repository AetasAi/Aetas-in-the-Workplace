What an engagement covers

Three areas of structured support

Every engagement begins with a no-cost discovery conversation and a structured diagnostic. What is introduced is built around your organisation — not a generic package applied by default.

01

Employee benefits consultancy

We review your pension and existing benefits to ensure they are structured effectively, clearly communicated, and genuinely valued by your employees. Where improvements are needed, we manage implementation on your behalf.

02

Financial resilience workshops

Practical financial education that replaces anxiety with clarity and capability. Covering the issues most likely to affect your workforce — from day-to-day money management to mid-career planning and retirement. One-to-one guidance is available where needed.

03

Wellbeing and incentive initiatives

Targeted programmes introduced through the Aetas Collective — our curated network of specialist partners — that strengthen engagement, culture, and performance. Introduced only where a clear need exists, and proportionate to your size.

Why Aetas ITW is different

Built for organisations where every employee is critical.

Most financial wellbeing solutions are designed for large corporate employers. They rely on broad platforms, generic content, and low-touch delivery. Scaled down, they do not translate well — and in a smaller organisation, the stakes are simply higher. One disengaged person in a 30-person team represents more than three percent of your entire workforce.

01

Always bespoke, never templated

Every engagement begins with a structured diagnostic before anything is introduced. No two programmes look the same, because no two organisations are the same.

02

Communication built in from the start

A benefit that is not understood is not working. Employee communications are managed throughout every engagement, not added as an afterthought.

03

Managed on your behalf

Aetas acts as a single point of coordination. You retain full visibility without being required to manage providers or produce communications yourself.

04

Commercially grounded

This is not a wellbeing initiative or a tick-box exercise. It is a structured, commercially justified approach to employee investment, built around performance and return.
