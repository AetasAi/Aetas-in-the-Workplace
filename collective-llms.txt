# Aetas Collective
> The specialist partner network within Aetas in the Workplace, designed to support employee financial wellbeing and improve Return on Employee Investment (ROEI) for SMEs and mid-sized organisations.

The Aetas Collective brings together carefully selected independent experts to deliver targeted, high-quality support where it adds genuine value — ensuring employers can access the right expertise without the complexity of managing multiple providers.

## What the Aetas Collective is

The Collective is the specialist partner network that sits within the Aetas in the Workplace (Aetas ITW) framework. It is not a directory of loosely affiliated businesses — it is a curated group of independently selected specialists, each chosen for their quality, relevance, and fit with the Aetas approach.

Partners deliver services directly to client organisations. Employers access the full breadth of Collective expertise through a single relationship with Aetas, without the complexity and cost of managing multiple providers independently.

## Ambassadors

Ambassadors are senior professionals who support Aetas in the Workplace in a non-commercial, introductory capacity. They are trusted voices who help introduce the programme to business leaders, share its principles, and open doors to meaningful conversations. Their role is one of advocacy, relationships, and reach.
